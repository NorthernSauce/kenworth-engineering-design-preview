+ Common questions

Before you send the drawing.

How fast will I get a price?

Honestly, anything quicker is a guess — and we don't do guesses. We aim to get a real quote back inside 48 working hours, with a delivery date, not a window.

What do you need from me to quote?

A drawing or model (DXF, DWG, STEP, IGES, PDF), the quantity, and a sentence on what the part is for. If something's missing we'll ask — we won't quote blind.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes. Routinely. Send yours with the RFQ or ask for ours.

What if my drawing isn't finished?

Send what you've got. If the geometry isn't manufacturable as drawn, you'll hear about it at quote stage — not three weeks into the job.

What sizes and volumes do you handle?

Prototype through to production batch. Envelope up to 1200 × 600 × 600 mm on 3-axis, 600 mm Ø on 5-axis. If your job isn't right for us we'll say so at quote stage.

Do you ship outside the UK?

Yes. ISO 9001:2015 docs, material certs, and inspection paperwork go in the box. Tell us the destination and we'll handle the freight side.
