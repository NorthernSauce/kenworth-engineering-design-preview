+ The story

Same shop. Thirty years.

Kenworth started in the early 1990s in Grimsby. Still in the same trade, still in the same town, still the same family. The work moved from manual to 5-axis CNC, dual-spindle turning, and a digital quality platform this summer. The standards haven't moved.

  1. 1992

    Kenworth Engineering founded in Grimsby. Manual machining, local customer base.

  2. 2000s

    First CNC investment. Manual work moves to programmed. Cycle times come down, repeatability goes up.

  3. 2010s

    ISO 9001:2015 certified. Second-generation family take operational lead.

  4. 2018

    Custom Hydrafeed Rota-Rack commissioned for the Citizen Miyano. Unmanned overnight running becomes routine.

  5. 2020

    DMG-Mori DMU-50 5-axis installed with the bespoke 20,000rpm Speed Master spindle. Single-setup work in-house.

  6. 2024

    Star SB20R sliding head added. Sliding-head capability moves in-house. No more subcontracting small-diameter long work.

  7. 2026

    Cloud manufacturing platform live across the shop floor. Job status, traceability, inspection — one system, every batch.

+ Three principles

What we believe.

01

No promises we can't keep.
