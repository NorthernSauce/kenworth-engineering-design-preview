+ Common questions

Before you send the drawing.

What tolerance can you hold on 5-axis?

Sub-10 micron on the DMU-50, documented per batch. At quote stage, you'll know which features will be tight and how we'll prove them.

What's a typical lead time?

Depends on geometry, material lead, and quantity. We aim to get a real date back inside 48 working hours — not a window.

Do you do small batches?

Yes. Prototype through to production volume. If the job isn't right for us, you'll know at quote stage.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes. Routinely.

What CAD formats do you accept?

DXF, DWG, STEP, IGES, and most standard formats. Got something else? Send it, we'll let you know.
